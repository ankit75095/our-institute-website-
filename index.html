What is the average salary range after doing the web designing course?

Web designers, salaries can start at around Rs. 25,000 per month. Experienced web designers, on the other hand, can earn anywhere from Rs. 30,000 to Rs. 1 lakh per month. Salaries of web designers also vary depending on the city one works in, with designers in Mumbai and Delhi usually earning more than those in smaller cities. This may vary across industries depending on the job role, skill, nature of work, city, etc.
